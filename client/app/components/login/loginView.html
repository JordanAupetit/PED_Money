MONEY IS DEAD, LONG LlVE MYMONEY

MyMoney is a personal finance management website, it allows you to consolidate all your accounts in one place to manage and organize your personal finances with ease, including the planning of your payments and managing your budget.

FEATURES

Your money

Each time you spend or make some money, add it to MyMoney website. The more you add information, the more MyMoney will be usefull and help you understand how you spend your money.

Budget

You want to save know how many money you kept this mounth to go to the restaurant today. We can help you. Let us know how much you want to spend and we will tell you if it's possible.

Works even offline

You go in a foreign country and want to keep your accounts up-to-date ? Don't worry about it. Basic features of MyMoney are still available while you are offline and will automatically be syncing when the network come back.

Free to use

MyMoney wants to help you to save your money. We think a lot and find right that using this webapp must be free for you and for every body.
